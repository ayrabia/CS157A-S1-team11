Core Features
Everything a Gym Needs
👤
Member Accounts
Members register, manage their profiles, view membership status (Active / Frozen / Expired), and track their activity — all from a single portal.
📅
Class Enrollment
Browse and enroll in fitness classes. Automatic waitlist management kicks in when capacity is full, with seamless cancellation support.
✅
Attendance Tracking
Staff check members in and out at the door. Every visit is logged with timestamps, including class-linked attendance for detailed history.
💳
Payment Processing
Record and track membership payments tied to plans. Supports Student Semester and VIP Access tiers with full transaction history.
🏋️
Trainer Management
Assign certified trainers to classes. Track specializations like CrossFit and Martial Arts with a Staff subclass model in the database.
🔐
Secure by Design
Passwords stored as cryptographic hashes. HTTPS enforced. SQL injection prevention with input validation and role-based access control throughout.
